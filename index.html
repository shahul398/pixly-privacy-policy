Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 29, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Pixly ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application Pixly ("App") handles your information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Pixly is designed with privacy in mind. The App:

  • Does NOT collect personal data
  • Does NOT require user registration or accounts
  • Does NOT track your location
  • Does NOT access your contacts
  • Does NOT use analytics or tracking tools
  • Does NOT contain advertisements

How the App Works

Pixly is an image editing application that allows you to resize, crop, compress, and convert images. Here's how we handle your data:

Photos and Images

  • The App accesses your device's photo gallery only when you explicitly select an image to edit
  • All image processing is performed locally on your device
  • Images are never uploaded to any external servers
  • Processed images are saved to your device's gallery with your permission

Storage Access

  • The App requires storage permission to:
    • Read images you select for editing
    • Save processed images to your gallery
  • We do not access any other files on your device

Internet Access

  • The App may use internet access for loading fonts
  • No user data is transmitted over the internet

Data Storage

  • All your images and edits remain on your device
  • We do not store any data on external servers
  • Uninstalling the App removes all App-related data from your device

Third-Party Services

Pixly does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.
